Save Elephants Mockup

Towards the end of my college career, I landed an internship several states away at 352media.com. The User Experience Director asked the design interns to create a website about something that inspired us.

I tried to pick a style that would require me to practice my drawing skills. Several of the items on my mockup were drawn then edited in Photoshop. Overall, I feel the design was good practice for a skill that needed some work.
