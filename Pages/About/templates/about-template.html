You know you’re doing it right when you annoy all your friends who aren’t designers.

I like to think I do this at my current job as a web designer at Healthx, a web portal company. I also use my other skills such as UX, web programming and writing. To keep from becoming stagnant, I try to throw in a challenge for myself in each project. Much of my work is varied in style. I like to call myself a “mixed bag,” due to starting in a journalism background.

However, I don’t work all the time (even though it feels like it some weeks). In my free time, I love playing the Sims, listening to Tool, or watching Design Star on HGTV. My biggest inspirations are from David Carson, Jan Tschichold and Salvador Dali.

I do try to attend as many design or UX meetup groups as possible, so please say hi if you see me there.
